﻿﻿Here I list a bunch of great CS self-learning materials that benifit me a lot.

This doc is for self-taught students who are like me, interested in CS while have no access to good education. For that, I am deeply grateful for those selfishless professors who share these excellent learning meterials for free. In return to their kindness, I personally maintain several curated lists that collect state-of-the-art papers and courses in my research fields, see my github homepage. I maintain a github repo to track the code and notes I learned in a course.

The eudcation backgourd only tells about the history. The course you took, the note your written, and the code you implemented make the future you who you are.

The whole point of doing so is for the democracy of knowledge.
